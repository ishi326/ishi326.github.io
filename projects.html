Projects

WEdecks: Ongoing project

WEdecks was created as a way to promote sports on the Wellesley campus in a fun and engaging way. The goal of this app is to build support for student athletes, increase attendance at games and keep the general Wellesley population aware of our amazing Varsity Teams.

BudgeEasy: Your Budgeting Buddy

BudgeEasy is a budgeting app that makes students' lives easier. It allows users to input thier monthly/weekly income and users get to decide how much to allocate to each domain of their life. BudgeEasy generates a plan that tracks their spending son that students don't need to worry about their expenses. Credit/Debit card statements can also be linked to the app.

World in the room

This project was created with the purpose of keeping places that are special to you, close to you. Each poster contains the name of the place, a quote and several pictures that make up the city.
